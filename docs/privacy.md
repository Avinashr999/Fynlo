# Fynlo Ledger Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 18 June 2026  
**Last updated:** 18 June 2026  
**App:** Fynlo Ledger  
**Package name:** app.fynlo

Fynlo Ledger is a personal finance ledger app for tracking accounts, expenses, loans, debts, investments, budgets, goals, and reports. This Privacy Policy explains what information Fynlo Ledger handles and how it is protected.

## Information you provide

Fynlo Ledger stores the information you enter into the app, including:

- Account names, balances, and account types
- Transactions, categories, notes, and dates
- Loan, debt, repayment, and interest records
- Investment names, values, source accounts, withdrawals, and related notes
- Budgets, goals, recurring entries, projects, and app preferences
- Optional profile/display name used inside the app

This financial information is used only to provide the app's ledger, reports, backup, restore, sync, and export features.

## Optional Google sign-in and cloud sync

You can use Fynlo Ledger without signing in. If you choose to sign in with Google, Fynlo Ledger uses Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore to sync your data to your own cloud account for backup and multi-device access.

When Google sign-in is used, Fynlo Ledger may receive your Google account identifier, name, and email address for authentication. Fynlo Ledger does not receive your Google password.

## Local storage

By default, Fynlo Ledger stores your app data on your device using Android local storage. Exports such as PDF, CSV, Excel, or backup files are saved only when you choose to create them. You are responsible for files after exporting or sharing them outside the app.

## Diagnostics and analytics

Fynlo Ledger may use Firebase services to understand app stability and performance, including crash reports, ANR reports, device model, Android version, app version, and basic usage events. Financial amounts, account balances, and detailed ledger entries are not intentionally sent to analytics.

## Information we do not sell

Fynlo Ledger does not sell your personal data or financial data. Fynlo Ledger does not use your financial records for advertising.

## Third-party services

Fynlo Ledger may use these Google/Firebase services:

- Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication for login
- Firebase Firestore for optional cloud sync
- Firebase Crashlytics for crash and ANR diagnostics
- Firebase Analytics and Firebase Performance for app quality and performance insights
- Google Play for app distribution and, if enabled later, billing

These services are governed by Google's privacy policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Permissions

Fynlo Ledger requests permissions only for app features, such as notifications, file export/import, biometric/PIN app lock, or other Android system functions required by the app. If a permission is optional, you can deny it, though the related feature may not work.

## Data deletion

You can reset local app data from inside Fynlo Ledger. If you use Google sign-in and cloud sync, you can also use the app's reset/sync tools or contact support for help with deletion requests.

## Children's privacy

Fynlo Ledger is intended for personal finance use by adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Fynlo Ledger changes features, data handling, or third-party services. The latest version will be available on this page.
