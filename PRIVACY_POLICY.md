# Privacy Policy for Fynlo

**Last Updated: April 24, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Fynlo** ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection & Privacy
Fynlo is a **manual entry personal ledger**.
*   **No Central Collection**: We do not collect, store, or share your financial data on our own servers. 
*   **User Ownership**: All financial records, notes, and transactions are yours. We have no access to your data.

## 2. Local Storage
All financial data you enter is stored **locally on your device** using a secure Room database. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up or export it.

## 3. Cloud Backups (Google Drive)
The App utilizes **Android Auto Backup** to protect your data against loss. 
*   If enabled on your device, your database is backed up to your **personal Google Drive** account managed by Google.
*   We do not have access to your Google Drive or the backup files stored there.

## 4. Manual Exports
The App allows you to manually export your data in JSON, CSV, or PDF formats. You are responsible for the security of these exported files once they leave the App.

## 5. Third-Party Services
*   **Firebase**: We use Firebase for app stability and basic performance monitoring. No personally identifiable financial information is sent to Firebase.
*   **Google Services**: The app uses Google Play Services for backup and restore functionality.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
